Guide for CodexCrusade25

1. Make a folder with your group's name ex. "snapdragon". save the answer sheet file in this folder

2. Participants can download the answer sheet (Answers.html) at any time during or after the challenge

3. Keep an eye on the timer at the top-right corner of the screen. This stage will end after the timer reaches 00:00

4. After the challenge ends, consult with your volunteer for further directions

5. You can reload the page if any problem occurs, this will not affect the timer

However DO NOT CLOSE THE TAB or you will be thrown out of the challenge and will have to start over

Best of luck guys :)
